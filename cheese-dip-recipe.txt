Ingredients:
- 1 block (32oz) of Velveeta (cubed)
- 2 cans of Rotel
- 1 pouound of favorite ground meat
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 block (8oz) of cream cheese (cubed)

Instructions:
1. Place Velveeta, Rotel, milk, and cream cheese in crockpot to melt.
2. Cook meat thoroughly, chopping it while in skillet.
3. Add meat to crockpot.
4. When all ingredients are melted and mixed, it is ready to serve.
